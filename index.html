What is the typical timeframe for building a villa in Hua Hin?

The timeframe can vary depending on the size and complexity of the project. Generally, after all permits are approved, a standard 3-bedroom pool villa takes approximately 10-14 months to complete.

Can foreigners legally own land in Thailand?

Foreign nationals cannot own land directly in their name. However, there are several common and secure legal structures, such as a long-term leasehold or setting up a Thai company. Our legal consultation, included in our service, will guide you through the safest and most suitable option for your situation.
